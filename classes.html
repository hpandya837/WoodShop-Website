HELLO

Classes

Intro to Woodoworking

Also Known As: Wood 1 Open To: All grades, 9-12 Full Year Course, 5 Credits Mr. D's Course Description: Wood 1 is designed to help emerge you into the skill of woodworking. You will be learning about shop safety and tools/ machine safety. The projects start off very basic in the beginning of the year and progressively get harder as you learn new woodworking skills. Projects Made in Wood 1: The Amish Picture Frame, The Candy Machine, The Jewelry Box, Ping Pong Paddle and Individual Projects. What Mr. D Expects of You: To come to class everyday with an open mind and willing to work. Also to treat people the way that you would like to be treated.

Cabinet Making

Also Known As: Wood 2 Open To: 10-12 Prerequisite: Intro To Woodworking (Wood 1) Full Year Course, 5 Credits Mr. D's Course Description: Wood 2 is designed to build off of Wood 1. In the 2nd level of woodworking you will be learning how to design and build bigger and more complex projects with more customization. By the end of the course you will be able to design a set a plan and build that project from scratch. Projects Made in Wood 2: The "Knock The Rust Off" Project, Flag Project, Lamp Project, Shaker Table, Ping Pong Paddle and Individual Projects. What Mr. D Expects of You: To come to class everyday with an open mind and willing to work. Also to treat people the way that you would like to be treated.

Advanced Cabinet Making

Also Known As: Adv. Woods Open To: 11-12 Prerequisites: Intro To Woodworking (Wood 1) & Cabinet Making (Wood 2) Full Year Course, 5 Credits Mr. D's Course Description: Adv. Woods is designed to build off the skills that you have learned in both Wood 1 and Wood 2. This course is more of an independent study and designed towards your project ideas. However there are two assigned projects that are mandatory for the class. Projects Made in Adv. Woods: The "Knock The Rust Off" Project, Adirondack Chair, Shop Improvement, Ping Pong Paddle and Individual Projects. What Mr. D Expects of You: To come to class everyday with an open mind and willing to work. Also to treat people the way that you would like to be treated.
