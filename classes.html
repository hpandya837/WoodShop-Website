Intro To Woodworking

Wood 1 introduces woodworking with a focus on shop and machine safety. Projects start simple and gradually increase in complexity as skills develop.

Cabinet Making

Wood 2 builds on Wood 1, teaching you to design and construct larger, more complex, and customizable projects. By the end, you'll be able to plan and build a project from scratch.

Advanced Cabinet Making

Adv. Woods expands on Wood 1 and 2, focusing on independent projects with two required assignments.
