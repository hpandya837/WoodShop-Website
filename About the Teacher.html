Mr. Dellapietro

Mr. DellaPietro, a dedicated woodworking instructor at Marlboro High School, was honored as the 2019-2020 Teacher of the Year for his exceptional teaching and commitment to student success. He teaches courses including Intro to Woodworking, Cabinetmaking, and Advanced Cabinetmaking, equipping students with valuable woodworking skills. Admired by students—one calling him "incredible" and "the best teacher I had in high school"—and respected by colleagues like Dr. Andreacci, who praised the positive environment he fosters, Mr. DellaPietro continues to have a lasting impact on the school community.

Professional Life

"Woodworking and teaching has been my passion for as long as I could remember. I can not think of any other thing I rather do for a career. To me, building something from nothing is one of the most satisfying things in the world. Especially when you step back and watch other people admire your work. But as a teacher, it is more rewarding watching my students make something with the knowledge I taught them. Then taking a step back and seeing the look on their face when they show off their work." - Mr. D
