Our Story

Every Toy Tells a Story

Little Bird Toys, established in 2018, brings families handcrafted wooden toys designed to spark imagination and slow down playtime. Our heirloom-quality collection is built from sustainable materials and made to last a lifetime, fostering connections between generations.

Why Choose Us

Our Strength

  • Quality

    We believe in the power of simple, safe play. Our handcrafted toys are free from plastic and batteries, and made with sustainable Baltic birch wood

  • Eco-Friendly

    At Little Bird Toys, we are committed to eco-friendly practices. Our wooden toys are designed for longevity, and our manufacturing process minimizes waste and supports the US economy.

  • Creative

    Our toys are designed to inspire imaginative and creative play. Free from batteries and screens, these toys encourage children to engage their creativity, develop motor skills, and enjoy endless hours of open-ended play.

  • Unique

    Little Bird Toys prides itself on offering beautifully handcrafted wooden toys made with attention to detail and superior craftsmanship. Each toy is meticulously designed and built to last, providing children with durable, high-quality playthings.
